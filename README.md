### 🧠 About My Vision
I am a Student and AI Researcher focused on breaking the boundaries of current machine learning paradigms. I believe that true Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) cannot be achieved simply by scaling Next-Token Prediction (Transformers). 

My research focuses on **Semantic State Evolution (SSE)**—building cognitive architectures that extract pure meaning and reason using fluid dynamics (Navier-Stokes) and non-linear ZULIC mathematics *before* translating thoughts into language.
